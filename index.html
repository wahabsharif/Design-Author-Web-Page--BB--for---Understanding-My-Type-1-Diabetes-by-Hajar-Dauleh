Understanding My Type 1 Diabetes

Dr. Hajar Dauleh

Join Ahmed and his best friends as they prepare for their school science fair with an exciting project about the human body. When Ahmed starts to feel unwell, he learns that he has diabetes. This unexpected discovery leads him on an enlightening journey, helping him and his friends understand how diabetes affects his body. Through vibrant illustrations and engaging narration, young readers will learn about insulin, blood sugar regulation, and how Ahmed manages his condition confidently. Understanding My Type 1 Diabetes offers a thoughtful introduction to diabetes, blending scientific discovery with heartwarming life lessons. It’s the perfect story for curious minds and future scientists!
